Detailed Description

template<typename T, std::size_t S>
class howl::ring_buffer< T, S >

Ring buffer on the stack, based on std::array. Aims to not rely on the heap and be a faster alternative than an std::vector containing raw pointers or unique_ptrs. It is meant for short lived objects as old ones get reused.
